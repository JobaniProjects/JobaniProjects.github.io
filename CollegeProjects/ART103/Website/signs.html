SIGNS & PLACE

Phase 1 | Sign Series




Sign 1

This location is at Bridgepoint, California, bordering Nevada. There has been frequent fires in California lately, and recently a gender reveal celebration is to
blame for another California wildfire. It would be funny to warn people coming to California to be cautious about gender reveal parties.







Sign 2

When taking a stroll around Upper Sierra in Santa Clara, I would see squirrels run across the streets to get from point a to point
b. In this road the cars go by really quickly, which can result in squirrel roadkill. Putting this sign would help bring awareness
to the drivers that there are squirrels in this area crossing the road.







Sign 3

This is William Street Park, close to the San Jose State University dorms. I usually come here to throw a ball or walk around with friends. What I did not
expect was that the park was popular with dog owners. Trying to throw a football or a frisbee around was impossible, because the dogs would try to catch it
too, and would leave your stuff chewed up. The sign would preemptively warn you of the dogs and how they will go for your ball.







Sign 4

At this location called Ocean Beach in San Francisco, when it is a sunny day there would be seagulls diving towards your food if left unguarded. This sign will warn you of the hungry
seagulls who will swoop down and try stealing your food.
