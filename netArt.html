NET ART



This project required a series of pages around a story using links, images and pages to tell the story in a modern, hypertext yet poetic way. My website is about a short adventure game that focuses on a person who yearns to go back home . You choose the actions of Jace as you explore the bizarre world he is stuck in and your only goal is to figure out how you will get out of this unfamiliar place. I was inspired by fallout because I love their interesting dialogue and detailed choice options that would impact the story. I incorporate these aspects in my website with the intention of you getting immersed in the game with dialogue and imagery.
