Exercise 4

For this Processing sketch, I wanted to recreate a place of relaxation in my home that helps soothe the mind after a long day. The bathtub was the perfect setting; calming music, bubbles, nice lighting, and the steam coming from the warm water was the environment I wanted to recreate. I used arrays to create the bubbles, steam clouds, and movement in different directions, bouncing from wall to wall.

Exercise 5

For exercise 5, I used arrays, OOP, pushMatrix, popMatrix, and my own created class called Smiley Face . To get the smiley faces go their own direction and change color, I needed to add loops, colorMode, and if else statements. I didn't know much about OOP at first, but through the process of this exercise I was able to understand it and apply it to my idea. The concept for this exercise was having a frog listening to music.

Project 2

For project 2, I used the fibonacci sequence to create the stars spiraling on the sky, the perlin noise algorithm to make the waves, as well as the millis command to make every method and class I created to execute in a specific time. Using time-based algorithms was interesting because it gave me the ability to control and show my methods in the order that I wanted it to appear.
