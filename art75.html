ART 75 | Intro to Digital Media





Artist Statement

My name is David Zenteno and my artwork incorporates a vibrant, colorful palette and hues to bring about the emotions of happiness, comfort, and warmth.
What I try to bring in my artwork is a euphoric experience while integrating psychedelic visuals. My interest in dreams and what visuals they produce helps me
explore ways to incorporate those traits into my work. I want my art to ease the mind of the viewer and be a therapeutic experience, taking away any stress in
the mind. Everyone has their special place where they get comfort and peace, and I am recreating that with what I envision. I create my artwork through digital
media, using Unity, HTML, CSS, Photoshop, Premiere, and Illustrator. Artists like Takashi Murakami and Tyler the Creator influence me to go towards vibrant
vivid colors. Aesthetics and the human desire for togetherness and tranquility inspire me to create artwork that sooths the soul.







Project 1



The video tells a story of how society has become dependent of technology, especially phones.
Sometimes people get caught up on technology that they miss parts of their life and do not
realize it until it is too late. In this situation, the main character is lucky enough to notice
the negative effects of the overconsumption of technology. He quickly breaks out the
cycle, but sometimes others are not as fortunate.










Project 2

Link to Project 2


I want my video installation to be about dreams. When the covid lockdowns began, I started sleeping more and dreaming more.
These dreams were intense and mind-bending, filled with distortion and vivid colors. I think due to the quick lifestyle change
because of the lockdown, my dreams were affected. This was my subconscious mind reacting to the stressful situation.
With Touch Designer and Premiere, I will alter the video to produce visuals of the dreams.









Project 3



This AR worlds lets you explore different worlds and dimensions in a blink of an eye. I want to show how powerful
technology has gotten for us to just experience something extraordinary through our phones. The robot is there
to show you these wonders and it rushes you to see everything, just like technology is rushing to advance and
we are trying to keep up.









Final Project



I decided to make a short scary film about a person walking to the park and also encounters a creature
who is out for blood. I filmed the main character walking, running, and tripping around a park
with my iphone and I used sounds from freesounds.org.
