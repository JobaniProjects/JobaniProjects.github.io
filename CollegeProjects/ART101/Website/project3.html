Project 3

For this project the objective was to create a clock in JavaScript with p5.js libraries and without the use of symbols or numbers. My clock has two modes, night mode and day mode. When it's 6 am, the background turns white and everything else turns black. When its 8pm, the background turns black and everything else turns white. The spinning rectangle in the middle keeps track of the milliseconds in real time. The rectangle tells you the hour; each line represents hours of 2, and every time the square is in between the lines, it represents an hour. The higher the rectangle is, the later the day is also. The circle shape is keeping track of the seconds. Every line of the circle section is representing 5 seconds that have passed. Once the circle reaches the right end of the canvas, 60 seconds have passed. The triangle tracks the minutes. When the left corner of the triangle touches the line, 2 minutes have passed. When the triangle reaches the left end of the canvas, sixty minutes have passed. I went for a minimalist look unintentionally because I found this project challenging, but I did learn a lot from it. I used functions like rotate, push, pop, randians, as well as new functions like map,milliseconds, seconds, minute, and hour.
