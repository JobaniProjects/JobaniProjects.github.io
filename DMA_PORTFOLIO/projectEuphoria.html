PROJECT EUPHORIA


Throughout life we experience emotional pain or trauma and there is nothing else you want to do but get away from the mental agony. You want peace of mind; you
want something that distracts you and brings you happiness. This is the emotion I’m trying to capture, pure ecstasy and tranquility. As human beings we strive for
protection and safety, and we tend to go towards the light rather than darkness for it, and that’s why I chose these warm colors and sunny setting, because nothing
sooths the mind better than nature and the summer sun that gives off that yellow orange hue. These colors convey optimism and warmth. Everyone
has their special place where they get comfort and peace and I am recreating that with what I find peaceful.
