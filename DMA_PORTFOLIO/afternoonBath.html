AFTERNOON BATH

For this Processing sketch, I wanted to recreate a place of relaxation in my home that helps soothe the mind after a long day. The bathtub was the perfect setting; calming music, bubbles, nice lighting, and the steam coming from the warm water was the environment I wanted to recreate. I used arrays to create the bubbles, steam clouds, and movement in different directions, bouncing from wall to wall.
