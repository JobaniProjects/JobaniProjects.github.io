DREAM MEMORY


My video installation for Art 75 was about dreams. When the covid lockdowns began, I started sleeping more and dreaming more. These dreams were intense
and mind-bending, filled with distortion and vivid colors. I think due to the quick lifestyle change because of the lockdown, my dreams were affected. This
was my subconscious mind reacting to the stressful situation. With Touch Designer and Premiere, I will alter the video to produce visuals of the dreams.
