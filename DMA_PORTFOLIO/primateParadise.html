PRIMATE PARADISE


During my childhood, one of my go to favorite videogame was Ape Escape 3. It was about troublesome monkeys that escaped into the world to cause chaos. The
stylish monkeys, the colorful scenes, and the humor of the game is what catpured my attention. This processing sketch is based on this childhood experience; I wanted
to create two monkeys encountering paradise, a temple of bananas. This sketch was difficult at first, because I didn't understand how to use pushMatrix and popMatrix,
but after some practice and tutorials, I was able to create this sketch by implementing custom functions, rotate, translate, arrays, pushMatrix, and popMatrix to make the
background bananas translate down and spinning, and to create the two monkeys.
