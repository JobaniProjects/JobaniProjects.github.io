Artist Statement

My name is David Zenteno and my artwork incorporates a vibrant, colorful palette and hues to bring about the emotions of happiness, comfort, and warmth.
What I try to bring in my artwork is a euphoric experience while integrating psychedelic visuals. My interest in dreams and what visuals they produce helps me
explore ways to incorporate those traits into my work. I want my art to ease the mind of the viewer and be a therapeutic experience, taking away any stress in
the mind. Everyone has their special place where they get comfort and peace, and I am recreating that with what I envision. I create my artwork through digital
media, using Unity, HTML, CSS, Photoshop, Premiere, and Illustrator. Artists like Takashi Murakami and Tyler the Creator influence me to go towards vibrant
vivid colors. Aesthetics and the human desire for togetherness and tranquility inspire me to create artwork that sooths the soul.
