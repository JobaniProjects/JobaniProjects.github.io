Project 4

Final

For the final project I created a game that incorporates flat design, gamification, and theatrification. I used p5.play, p5.clickable, p5.sound, p5.scenemanager, and p5.js. All the flat design art that was used was created by me through Adobe Illustrator. The objective of the game is to disarm the machine that's causing all the burning in the forest. First you have to maneuver your way to the machine without getting caught by the enemy spirits. This assignment was fun and challenging but due to a busy schedule I was not able to fully flesh out the game.
