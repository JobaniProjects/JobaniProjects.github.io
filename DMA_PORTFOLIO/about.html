ABOUT ME

My name is David Zenteno and I am a Mexican-American student attending San Jose State University. I plan to become a game designer because videogames were a main part of my childhood; seeing these unique created worlds, these intricate storylines and characters come to life as if it actually existed, sparked my passion. Itâ€™s imagination coming to life and having the ability to share your vision with others. I believe that pursuing Digital Media Art will help me reach this goal, providing me with vast knowledge. My artwork incorporates a vibrant, colorful palette and hues to bring about the emotions of happiness, comfort, and warmth. What I try to bring in my artwork is a euphoric experience while integrating psychedelic visuals. My interest in dreams and what visuals they produce helps me explore ways to incorporate those traits into my work. I want my art to ease the mind of the viewer and be a therapeutic experience, taking away any stress in the mind.
Everyone has their special place where they get comfort and peace, and I am recreating that with what I envision. I create my artwork through digital media, using Unity, HTML, CSS, Java, Javascript, Photoshop, Premiere, and Illustrator. Artists like Takashi Murakami and Tyler the Creator influence me to go towards vibrant vivid colors. Game designer Hideo Kojima inspired me to take these works of art and create intricate stories around them. Aesthetics and the human desire for togetherness and tranquility inspire me to create artwork that soothes the soul.
