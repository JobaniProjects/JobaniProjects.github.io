For the first assignment, we were given three strategies to choose from: "What would your closest friend do?", "Decorate, decorate", and "What are the sections, sections of? Imagine a catepillar moving." We all agreed to pick "What would your closest friend do?" as our strategy and we all used paper as our medium.
